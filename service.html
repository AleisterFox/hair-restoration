Injerto Capilar Manual (FUE)



El Injerto Capilar Manual (Follicular Unit Extraction) consiste en extraer unidades foliculares (familias de pelos) de una zona donde exista buena densidad, e implantarlos a una zona donde exista escasez.


Manual Hair Graft (Follicular Unit Extraction) consists of extracting follicular units (families of hairs) from an area where there is good density, and implanting them in an area where there is scarcity.
